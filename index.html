About Me

Hello! I'm Patrick Knowles. I have a bachelor of science in physics and a minor in general science from Bowling Green State University. I am currently in a master of science program in mechanical engineering at the University of Colorado and am expecting to graduate in the spring of 2027! I have a deep interest in applying computational tools to aid experimental research. But with this, I also enjoy learning about various different fields such as complex systems, social science, sports science, etc.

My previous experience is largely simulating materials using a variety of software, designing an optical system at Los Alamos National Laboratory, and conducting experimental research on quantum dot synthesis. As of today, I would like to apply my knowledge in creating practical solutions toward problems facing humanitarian efforts. An example of this would be to develop cheaper alternatives to established but deeply unfair norms. Such as farmers facing incredible financial strain due to modern machine costs or creating a microscope with cost-effective materials to help doctors in disadvantaged areas detect diseases faster. I am also interested in aiding fundemental research efforts through computational prediction!

Beyond my research and interests, I possess strong skills in Python, C++, and various laboratory techniques, coupled with soft skills in effective communication and team leadership. I'm always eager to collaborate on innovative projects. Let's connect!
